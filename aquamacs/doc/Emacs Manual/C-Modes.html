30.12 C and Related Modes

This section gives a brief description of the special features available in C, C++, Objective-C, Java, CORBA IDL, Pike and AWK modes. (These are called “C mode and related modes.”) See CC Mode, for a more extensive description of these modes and their special features.

  • Motion in C: Commands to move by C statements, etc.
  • Electric C: Colon and other chars can automatically reindent.
  • Hungry Delete: A more powerful DEL command.
  • Other C Commands: Filling comments, viewing expansion of macros, and other neat features.
